Bluetooth Pairing

Customer’s Return Reason : Cannot pair the headphones with a device
1

- With the charging case lid open, press and hold the pairing button on the back of the charging case for about 5 seconds or more.

Batterry issue

the earbuds are not charging /not working
1

Remove plastic film over charging pins

2

- Check the direction of the earbuds

- Insert them into the grooves of the left and right slots of the charging case.

3

- Check the charging cable.

4

When the battery is completely discharged, the earbuds require at least 10 minutes of charging time to turn on.

One earbud won't connect

Customer’s Return Reason : The right/left earbud not working
1

- Unpair the earbuds from your phone by removing them from the Bluetooth list and turn off your phone’s Bluetooth.

2

Power off both earbuds by pressing and holding each one for 5 seconds.

Reset the earbuds by pressing and holding the controls simultaneously for 10 seconds.

3

Put the earbuds in the charging case

4

Pair the earbuds with your phone by turning on your Bluetooth and finding your Skullcandy model in the discovered devices list

No Power

1

The remaining battery of the headphones is 1% or lower. Charge the headphones and check if the indicator lights up.

2

- Make sure the battery is fully charged.

3

If the issue persists, Perform a Factory Reset.

Factory Reset

Customer’s Return Reason : connection problems, Batterry issue, synchronization...
1

- After about 15 seconds, the indicator (orange) on the charging case flashes for about 5 seconds.

- Then, the indicator on the charging case turns off and the headset will be reset.
