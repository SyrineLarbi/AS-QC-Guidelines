Batterry issue

Customer’s Return Reason : Battery issue (The battery stops holding its charge.)
1

- Type cmd into the search bar

- Type this command: powercfg /batteryreport

Open the file by copying and pasting the path(copy it from the Command Prompt) into your browser :

2

- Design Capacity is the original maximum charge of your battery.

- Full Charge Capacity is how much charge your laptop battery is capable of holding now.

If these two numbers are pretty close, then you have a healthy battery.

But if the Full Charge Capacity is much lower than the Design Capacity, then your battery's health has dropped significantly.
