3

3- Check that the TV Energy Saving Switch (at the back n°6) is ON

Remote Control Issues
1

Check for physical damage

2

Slow Response Time : Remove any kind of obstacle

3

1- Remove the batteries from the remote


2- Press the power button of the remote for three seconds


3- Install the batteries into the remote, and try using it again

There is no sound, poor sound, or low volume coming from the TV speakers.
1

Try increasing the volume of your TV.

Make sure the TV isn't muted.

Try performing a soft reset

2

If there is still no sound, reset the TV to factory settings.

Physical damage
1

Firstly, Always do a Visual inspection of the packaging

If it has any signs of damages or wet.

2

Then Check your TV screen for physical damage (dents, tears, cracks, etc.).

Flickering screen/Lines
1

If you can't observe any physical damage.

Turn off the TV, keep it unplugged for 30 seconds and Plug it back in to turn it on again.

2

Check the cable connection or try a different cable

3

Update the TV software to the latest version

3

Reset the TV to the original factory settings
