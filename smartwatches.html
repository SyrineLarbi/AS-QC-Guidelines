Enable notification

Customer’s Return Reason : I’m not getting notifications on my watch.
1

- Make sure your phone’s Bluetooth is on and your smartwatch is synced to your device.

- Check you haven't enabled Notify App silence mode, or any sleeping time.

2

- check that notifications are enabled

- Check you haven't enabled Notify App silence mode, or any sleeping time.

3

Open Settings. Tap Apps and notifications > Notifications.

- Tap the app name and turn notifications ON

Screen failure

Customer’s Return Reason : If your watch’s display has streaks, appears to be pixelated, or flickers
1

- Force Restart

- Continue holding the buttons when the power screen appears.

- When the screen goes black, release the Home and back buttons, and your watch will automatically restart.

2

Check for software updates.

3

Reset the smartwatch to the original factory settings

Batterry issue

Customer’s Return Reason : refuses to charge, the battery is draining so fast
1

- Check for damage to the charger or the charging cable. If you find any, use an alternative.

Check for software updates.
